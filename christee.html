About

This website is an archive of the software and hardware for the NASA Robotic/Lunar Mining team at the University of Akron. We have put together this website to collectively hold the work that we performed on our robots for the years of 2012, 2013, and 2014. We have posted out work so that others can learn from the materials and so that other teams participating in the future can learn from what we learned throughout our years participating in the compeition.

So take a look at the various tabs that house our past robots. On each of the pages we have outlined the challaneges that each of the designs had and what we learned from each of them. There are links to designs and code for the robot as well on each of the pages. All of our designs and code have been comitted to Github to help ease finding the material.

If you have any questions feel free to ask us! You can reach us by checking out our group Github and reaching out to one of the members of the group.
